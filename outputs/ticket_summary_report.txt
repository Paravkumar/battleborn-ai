    **Practical steps for using your AuraSync earbuds while drinking water**

1. **Check the rating**  
   - *AuraSync Basic*: **IPX4** – protects against splashes and light rain.  
   - *AuraSync Pro*: **IPX6** – protects against stronger water jets (e.g., heavy rain, sweat‑intense workouts).

2. **Stay within the rating**  
   - For IPX4, avoid submerging the earbuds or exposing them to direct streams of water (e.g., shower, pool).  
   - For IPX6, you can tolerate brief exposure to higher‑pressure water (e.g., sweaty workouts), but still avoid full immersion.

3. **Use a secure fit**  
   - Ensure the earbuds stay in place so water doesn’t force its way into the speaker mesh.

4. **Dry them promptly**  
   - After exposure to water, wipe the exterior with a soft, dry cloth and let the earbuds air‑dry before storing them in the case.

5. **Avoid high‑pressure sources**  
   - Do not point high‑pressure water (e.g., from a faucet or hose) directly at the earbuds, even on the IPX6 model.

6. **Know the warranty limits**  
   - Water damage is covered **only** if it stays within the stated IP rating. Damage from submersion beyond IPX4/IPX6, or from non‑certified accessories, voids the warranty.

Follow these steps to enjoy your earbuds while staying hydrated without risking damage or voiding the warranty.
